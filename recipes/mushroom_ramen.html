15-Minute Mushroom Instant Ramen Recipe

oh no go back

This isn’t your typical instant ramen noodle recipe. What sets it apart are the hearty mushrooms which are beautifully browned to amplify the earthy mushroom flavors and aroma. I also added a few seasoning ingredients like scallions, homemade broth, and chili peppers to layers of flavor. Best of all, this recipe comes together in no time. With just a couple of additional ingredients and a few minutes of prep time, you’ll be able to enjoy a hot steaming bowl of mushroom ramen in the comfort of your home, in just 15 minutes!

  • 1 pack instant ramen (I like Shin Ramen)
  • 500-525 ml (2 1/4 cup) broth or water
  • 8 oz assorted mushrooms I used enoki, shiitake, oyster mushrooms
  • 1 chopped scallions
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 sliced chili pepper
  1. Start by prepping the mushrooms. Slice the shiitake mushrooms to 1/2 inch piece. Rip the enoki and oyster mushrooms into smaller pieces.
  2. Heat a pot over medium heat. Add shiitake and oyster mushrooms, and cook until charred and browned on both sides.
  3. Add neutral oil then toss in the white part of the scallions. Add 1/4 of the instant ramen seasoning packet and quickly stir into the mushrooms and scallions to prevent the seasoning from burning.
  4. After 30 seconds, pour in the broth or water and bring to a boil. Add the remaining instant ramen seasoning packet, noodles, and dried vegetable flakes (if any).
  5. Add enoki mushrooms, egg, green part of the scallions and sliced pepper. Cook the noodles to your desired doneness - I recommend cooking it about 30 sceonds to 1 minute less than the recommended cooking instructions for al dente.
